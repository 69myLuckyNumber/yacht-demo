Bluefish It's a bit late in the season and generally the bluefish have moved offshore by now looking for bait and cooler waters, but we recently got into some good action over at Wasque. The bluefish were of good size and, as always, a lot of fun to catch for our guests. Parachute jigs of varying color seemed to work best. We tried casting Romano plugs and hooked a few off the breakers, but much more action jigging at 2-4 knots. We did need to wait for the tide to turn before the bite picked up.

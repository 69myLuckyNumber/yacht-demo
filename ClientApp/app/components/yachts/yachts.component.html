Quite Time

Quiet Time is a beautiful 37' Sea Ray motor yacht that is a joy to cruise. She has ample of space inside and out and boats powerful engines to get you to your destination quickly and in comfort. The boat is fully air conditioned and has a roomy toilet with shower. A cockpit shower is available to freshen up after swimming

List of features: BBQ, generator, A/C, showers, bathroom, TV/DVD, bow sunpads

Mako

This little battlewagon is great for inshore and near-shore fishing, although we've been known to go offshore for tuna on it, too. This sturdy 20' Mako center console is available for rental as well as guided fishing and lobstering charters.
